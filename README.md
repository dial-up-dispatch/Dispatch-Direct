# Dispatch Direct

> *Tools that live beside you.*

Dispatch Direct is the browser extension arm of **Dial Up Dispatch**: a small catalog of sidebar tools built to stay out of your way while you work, think, and exist on the internet. Each one lives at the edge of your attention, does its job, and leaves your data exactly where it belongs: with you.

---

## 📡 The Catalog

### 01 · Brain Dump Inbox
**Status: Live: v1.0.0**
**Browsers: Opera · Chrome · Vivaldi**

A local-first thought capture tool for the browser sidebar. When something surfaces mid-browse: an idea, a task, a feeling, a reminder: you reach sideways, dump it, tag it, and get back to what you were doing. CRT terminal aesthetic, four phosphor themes, typewriter click sounds, a personality feed that reacts to everything you do. Export your entire pile as Markdown anytime. Nothing leaves your browser.

*The keeper. Catches what's worth keeping.*

→ [`/brain-dump-inbox`](./brain-dump-inbox/)

---

### 02 · The Void
**Status: Active Development: v1.0**
**Browsers: Opera**

A private release space for the thoughts you don't want to keep. You type something, press Enter, and watch it dissolve. The Void stores nothing: genuinely, architecturally nothing. Three signal atmospheres, generative ambient audio, a dissolution ritual that makes the release feel real.

*The releaser. Consumes what needs to go.*

→ [`/the-void`](./the-void/)

---

### 03 · Brain Dial
**Status: Idea Seed 🌱**

The grown-up successor to Brain Dump Inbox: a full focus terminal for the sidebar. Pomodoro timer, thought capture, prose station, session history, and a personality feed that reacts to all of it.

*The focuser. Holds the session together.*

---

### 04 · Signal Lost
**Status: Idea Seed 🌱**

An ambient presence for the sidebar: generative audio, cryptic CRT fragments cycling on screen, the feeling of tuning into a dead channel from somewhere. Not utility. Atmosphere.

*Presence is the feature.*

## 🧬 The Promise and the Answer

Every Dispatch Direct extension is an answer and a promise.

The promise never changes: your data is always yours, and always accessible. Even if this project disappeared tomorrow, you could open a folder and find everything you ever put into it, in a format any text editor can read. That's not a feature. That's a commitment.

The answer is different every time. Sometimes it fills a gap. Sometimes it solves a problem. Sometimes it just makes someone's day a little better, or scratches a creative itch, or exists because building it taught something worth knowing. Any of those is enough.

That's the whole brief.

## 🎨 Visual Identity

All brand assets: logos, icons, promo tiles, profile pictures: are generated from code. SVG and HTML Canvas, no design software. The brand is built the same way the tools are built. Icon generation files live in each extension's `/references` folder.
